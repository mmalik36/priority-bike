The Priority Fold

A Priority Bike Product

Our Priority Fold has a lightweight, stripped down, simple but robust design, like our previous products, lowering testing costs. The bike can fold easily, allowing customers to save space at home in their apartment or when taking a crowded train. The accessories will be sold separately, allowing a cheap initial purchase and later customization when the customer feels comfortable with it.

Join the fun!

Participate in alpha and beta testing!

Alpha testing consists of trying out the base model of the Priority Fold without any attachment. The expected trial period will run for 3 weeks once invited to participate. Beta testing consists of testing the advanced model of the Priority Prototype Fold with attachments (Click here for attachment info!). The expected trial period will run for 3 weeks and once successfully completed you become eligible for early access for the prototype model.
